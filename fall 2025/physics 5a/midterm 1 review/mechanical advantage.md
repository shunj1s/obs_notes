$$\vec{F}_{net} = 2T - mg$$ $$ma = 2T-mg = 2F_p - mg$$
$$ 0 = 2T - mg$$
thus, the person can lift M if
$$ F_p \leq \frac{mg}{2}$$
there exists a tradeoff in Atwood machines!
